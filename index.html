Public Speaking

My mission as a paid public speaker: I help leaders build intentional relationships to create a never-want-to-leave workplace.

I deliver high-impact, high-value keynotes that amuse, inform, stimulate, reach deep, and challenge to action. And that's just in the first 7 minutes.

Dare to Be an Intentional Leader: Create a Never-Want-To-Leave Workplace
Do your employees dream of coming to work or dread it? Do employee issues keep you awake? This keynote sparks ideas like: How you to create a brilliant work place? A place where employees contribute meaningful cost-saving ideas and higher profits. How do you create a place where you enjoy working with people who enjoy working with you? Answer: create Intentional Managers who build relationships. Who energize employees. Who live and lead by the motto: "It's not just business, it's personal." Intentional Leaders are servant-leaders, not lip-service-leaders. Intentional Leaders don't settle for old, tired, traditional ways of solving business issues.

How to Create Intentional Employees: Developing Contributors to a Never-Want-to-Leave Workplace
Intentional Employees are seldom hired. You create them. Intentional Employees know things. Yes, they know their jobs. But, more importantly, they know they are valued, are listened-to, and they matter. Intentional Employees are engaged, contribute ideas, and improvements. Some develop into Intentional Managers who perpetuate your never-want-to-leave workplace culture. Learn how to create Intentional Employees in this fast-moving, seismic reinvention of who leads and who follows.

Heroes and Inspirations: Living Up to the Respect of Your Employees Have you ever thought deeply about who your Heroes and Inspirations are? Who impacted your life so greatly that you remember them, maybe even live your life to imitate them? Who's someone who made a deep, lasting impression on your life? Now, think about your managers and employees. Who are their Heroes and Inspirations? Each of them have one, just like you do. Is it someone in your company? As research for this keynote for your conference or company, I will ask your members or employees. I will ask them to write a short tribute or salute to their individual Hero or Inspiration. I will pull quotes from some of these and highlight them in my talk. You will be surprised, delighted, and amazed by who they hold up as Heroes and Inspirations. And what they say will reveal the real culture and values of your organization. Find out what they say. If you dare.
